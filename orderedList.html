Types of Triangle

Scalene Triangle
Acute-Angled Triangle (Acute Triangle) A triangle is considered to be an equilateral triangle when all three sides have the same length.
Isosceles Triangle
When two sides of a triangle are equal or congruent, then it is called an isosceles triangle.
Equilateral Triangle
When none of the sides of a triangle are equal, it is called a scalene triangle.
